# Step 03: Upgrade NL/EN Localization and Copy Quality

## Objective

Create high-quality localized copy with consistent terminology and native tone.

## Inputs

- `src/i18n/messages/en.ts`
- `src/i18n/messages/nl.ts`
- `src/app/(public)/about/page.tsx`
- `src/app/(public)/faq/page.tsx`

## Tasks

1. Define terminology guide (EN + NL):
   - choose preferred terms for bike fitting, fit report, position, measurement, etc.
2. Remove mixed-language phrasing where NL alternatives are stronger.
3. Normalize tone and sentence rhythm for readability.
4. Align FAQ, About, and homepage vocabulary.
5. Add editorial QA checklist for future content updates.

## Deliverable

- EN/NL terminology style guide
- Rewritten NL/EN copy on key pages with consistent wording

## Completion Checklist

- [ ] Terminology guide is documented.
- [ ] NL copy reads naturally (not literal English translation).
- [ ] EN and NL have content parity but are culturally adapted.
- [ ] Spelling/grammar checks completed.
